About Armapel

Armapel is a dynamic technology company and web developer at the forefront of the Philippines' digital transformation. The company's identity is deeply rooted in the vision of its founder, whose name forms the very essence of Armapel (Ar-Ma-Pel). This signifies a personal commitment to excellence in every project, from enterprise-level solutions to public-facing platforms.

Corporate History

November 2024

The company was founded with the launch of its initial product, lingk IPTV, an M3U link hosted on GitHub.

December 2024

Service for the M3U link was suspended after a key channel from the Philippines became unavailable.

February 2025

The company resumed operations with the launch of a new web-based platform, Lingk, streaming Cignal Play channels.

April 2025

A significant corporate rebranding was undertaken, changing the company name to Flow Streaming Co., Ltd. and launching Flow+ and FlowNews.

May 2025

FlowNews was discontinued due to technical issues, and the TMDB partnership for Flow+ was terminated.

July 2025

The company rebranded a second time to Armapel, launching Uzer! and rebranding the core streaming service as Liveplay.

August 2025

The Lingk brand was reintroduced with a new global website hosting an M3U link for international channels.
